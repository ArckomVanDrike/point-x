About Point-X

World X is a powerful tool for precise GPS positioning and location detection. It utilizes the Open Location Code (Plus Code) system to provide accurate and unique location codes for any point on the Earth's surface, regardless of traditional addressing systems.

With Point-X, you can easily find and share your exact location using the Plus Code, a globally recognized and user-friendly format. Whether you are a topographer, geographer, or an outdoor enthusiast, Point-X empowers you with the ability to precisely communicate geographic locations.

Point-X is designed to be simple, efficient, and accessible for everyone. It is an essential tool for anyone who needs to work with GPS coordinates and Plus Codes, making it an invaluable asset in various fields of geography, cartography, and location-based services.
